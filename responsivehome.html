Alternative dispute resolution (ADR) is a term that is generally used to refer to informal dispute resolution processes where parties meet with a professional third party who helps them solve their disputes in a way that is less formal than the court system

Due to the highly litigious state of the Federation, many claimants have to wait for a decade or more for a verdict, which may then be subject to an appeal. A typical court case takes between 2 and 20 years to conclude
