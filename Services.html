Have you heard of YOA?

YOA is a distinct feature of everyday life in all modern societies. No matter how barbaric or primitive, every society has certain set of rules which are developed overtime to regulate the social activities so as to bring about peaceful coexistence among its members. These set of rules as long as they enjoy obligation are laws and they have a binding force on all members of such society. Glandville Williams in ‘Learning the law’ opines that law is the cement of the society which synchronizes all the parts of the society in a harmonious way. Accordingly, any society devoid of law and order will be fraught with crisis on the basis of survival of the fittest.
